Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

Find answers to commonly asked questions about Dots Consultants

How do I download and get started with the Dots Consultant App?

You can download the Dots Consultant App from our website. Once downloaded, follow the registration process and complete your profile to start receiving job requests.

What industries does Dots cover?

Dots covers a wide range of industries, including agriculture, mining, manufacturing, technology, and more. You can choose which industries you're skilled in during the sign-up process.

How and when do I get paid?

Consultants receive payment for each job they complete to their App Wallet. Payments are then processed every 14th night directly to your account.

Can I select multiple industries?

Yes, you can select multiple industries you're skilled in, and rate your expertise from Level 1 (Basic Knowledge) to Level 3 (High Level of Expertise).

Can I decline job requests?

Yes, you are free to decline any job that you are unable to take. Declining a job won't affect your profile or future job offers.
